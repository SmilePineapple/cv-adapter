## 📋 FAQ Answers (For directory Q&A sections)

**Q: Is CV Buddy really free?**
```
Yes! CV Buddy offers a completely free tier that lets you create tailored CVs with no sign-up required. We also offer a Pro plan with additional features like unlimited generations and premium templates.
```

**Q: How does the AI tailoring work?**
```
Our AI (powered by GPT-4) analyzes both your CV and the job description, then intelligently adapts your CV to highlight relevant skills, experience, and keywords that match the role. It maintains your authentic experience while optimizing for ATS systems and recruiter preferences.
```

**Q: What is an ATS and why does it matter?**
```
ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software that 75% of companies use to filter CVs before humans see them. Our tool ensures your CV passes these systems by using proper formatting, relevant keywords, and optimized structure.
```

**Q: Do I need to sign up?**
```
No! You can try CV Buddy completely free without creating an account. Just visit the website, upload your CV, and start creating tailored versions.
```

**Q: What file formats do you support?**
```
You can upload CVs in PDF, DOCX, or TXT format. You can download your tailored CV as DOCX or PDF.
```

**Q: Is my data secure?**
```
Yes. We use enterprise-grade security with Supabase and don't share your data with third parties. You can delete your data anytime.
```
