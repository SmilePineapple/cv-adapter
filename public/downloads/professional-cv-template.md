## INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE:

### 1. Replace All Placeholder Text
- Delete all text in [brackets] and replace with your information
- Remove any sections that don't apply to you
- Keep it to 2 pages maximum

### 2. Tailor for Each Application
- Adjust Professional Summary to match the job
- Reorder bullet points to highlight relevant experience first
- Add keywords from the job description
- Remove irrelevant experience/skills

### 3. Quantify Everything
- Add numbers: percentages, revenue, team sizes, timeframes
- Use the formula: Action + Task + Result with numbers
- Example: "Increased sales by 35% through data-driven marketing strategy"

### 4. Use Action Verbs
Start bullet points with: Led, Managed, Achieved, Implemented, Increased, Reduced, Developed, Created, Launched, Delivered, Improved, Streamlined, Optimized, Generated, Built, Established

### 5. Format Consistently
- Use the same date format throughout (Month Year)
- Keep bullet point style consistent
- Maintain consistent spacing
- Use professional font: Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman (10-12pt)

### 6. ATS Optimization
- Use standard section headings
- No tables, text boxes, or columns
- No graphics or images
- Save as .docx or PDF
- File name: FirstName_LastName_CV.pdf

### 7. Proofread
- Check for typos and grammatical errors
- Read aloud to catch mistakes
- Ask someone else to review
- Verify all dates and contact information

## TIPS FOR DIFFERENT EXPERIENCE LEVELS:

### Graduates (0-2 years):
- Focus more on education section (include modules, dissertation, societies)
- Include part-time jobs, internships, volunteering
- Highlight transferable skills
- Use projects and university achievements
- 1 page is acceptable

### Mid-Level (3-10 years):
- Focus on work experience
- Education brief (just degree and grade)
- 5-6 bullets for current role, 3-4 for previous roles
- Quantify all achievements
- 2 pages standard

### Senior (10+ years):
- Focus on strategic achievements and leadership
- Summarize or omit roles from 15+ years ago
- Education one line only
- Emphasize business impact and team leadership
- 2 pages (3 if extensive senior experience)
