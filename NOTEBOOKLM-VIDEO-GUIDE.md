## 🎙️ What is NotebookLLM Audio Overview?

NotebookLLM's "Audio Overview" feature converts documents into **engaging 8-15 minute podcast-style discussions** between two AI hosts. The hosts:

- Have natural, conversational dialogue
- Break down complex topics into digestible insights
- Use analogies and examples
- Sound genuinely enthusiastic and curious
- Create a "deep dive" feel without being dry

**Why it works for CV Buddy:**
- Explains the problem (ATS systems, CV tailoring) in an engaging way
- Demonstrates value without being salesy
- Builds trust through education
- Perfect for homepage hero section
- Shareable on social media

3. **Generate Audio Overview**

   - Download the audio file (MP3)

4. **Create Video**
   - Use the audio as voiceover
   - Add visuals in video editor (see Visual Strategy below)
   - Export as MP4

**Pros:**
- ✅ Free
- ✅ Natural-sounding AI voices
- ✅ Engaging conversational style
- ✅ Quick to generate (5-10 mins)

**Cons:**
- ❌ Less control over exact script
- ❌ Need to create source document first

### CV Buddy: The AI CV Builder Solving the ATS Problem

**The Problem**

75% of CVs are rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) before reaching human recruiters. These automated systems filter applications based on keywords, formatting, and relevance to job descriptions.

Job seekers are advised to tailor their CV for every application, but this process takes 2-3 hours per job. For someone applying to 10 positions, that's 20-30 hours of manual work.

**The Solution**

CV Buddy is an AI-powered CV builder that automates the tailoring process. Users upload their CV once, paste any job description, and receive a perfectly tailored, ATS-optimized CV in 2 minutes.

**How It Works**

1. Upload existing CV (any format: PDF, DOCX, TXT)
2. Paste target job description
3. AI analyzes both documents using GPT-4
4. Generates tailored CV highlighting relevant skills and experience
5. Download in DOCX or PDF format

- AI-powered CV tailoring using OpenAI GPT-4
