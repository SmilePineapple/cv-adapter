## ⚠️ SEO Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Keyword Stuffing** - Write for humans first, search engines second
2. **Duplicate Content** - Each page needs unique content
3. **Ignoring Mobile** - 60%+ traffic is mobile
4. **Slow Page Speed** - Kills rankings and conversions
5. **No Internal Linking** - Connect your pages together
6. **Thin Content** - Aim for 1,500+ words on key pages
7. **Ignoring User Intent** - Match content to what users want
8. **No Schema Markup** - Missing rich snippets opportunity
9. **Broken Links** - Check and fix regularly
10. **Not Tracking Results** - Can't improve what you don't measure
