## ⚠️ Important Notes

1. **Avoid Paid Directories** - The ones you mentioned (Product Hunt featured, AlternativeTo premium, etc.) are paid. Focus on free tiers only.

2. **Quality Over Quantity** - Better to have 50 quality listings than 200 spam directories.

3. **Consistent NAP** - Use the same Name, Address, Phone across all directories for SEO.

4. **Monitor Backlinks** - Use Google Search Console to track new backlinks.

5. **Update Regularly** - Keep listings fresh with new screenshots, updated descriptions.

6. **Respond to Reviews** - If directories allow reviews (G2, Capterra), respond to all reviews.
